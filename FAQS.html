Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is the return policy?

Answer 1: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund.

Question 2: Do you offer free shipping?

Answer 2: Yes, we offer free shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat shipping fee of $5 applies.

Question 3: How long does it take to process an order?

Answer 3: Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Once your order is processed, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information.

Question 4: Can I track my order?

Answer 4: Yes, you can track your order using the tracking number provided in the shipping confirmation email. You can also track your order on our website using the 'Order Tracking' feature.

Question 5: What payment methods do you accept?

Answer 5: We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
